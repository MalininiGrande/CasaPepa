About Me

Ever since I was a kid, I have always loved to plan things. Oh, the things I planned! From English classes for my plush toy friends and crazy action-packed ventures with my human friends to my grandparents quit smoking regimes and my mom's love life. This love and almost a physical need to plan sticked with me to my adulthood and steered me into a direction which enables me to do what I love and find the most sense in for a living...and here I am.

Luckily I have grown and can know use my mad planning skills for people that are actually benefiting from it. Starting my career as a registration coordinator and later event assistant with GUARANT Intl. company was a great school for me. Apart from organizing small events for dozens of people to huge events of several thousands, I have discovered that I am not only a great planner, but I am super resilient to stress, can think clearly even under pressure, can communicate with almost anyone even if we do not speak common language, am a team player, although need a certain level of independence and decision making powers and much more!

When I moved to my current job, Activities Associate in the International School of Prague, I discovered one more passion - service and volunteering. Why would not anyone want to make world a better place, right? This aspect of planning makes the final product or event even more rewarding not only for me but also for the participants, therefore lately I have been trying to sneak in all those little service parts (runs, community helps, service challenges etc.) and really make a difference. My wild imagination and knowledge of many online tools and software helps my greatly in this effort.
